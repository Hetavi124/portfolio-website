SECTION 03

Email & CRM

Outbound engines, lifecycle flows and CRM hygiene: email built for the inbox it actually lands in.

// INOBAL Saleshandy · Mailchimp · Brevo · HubSpot 2025 → Present

A cold outbound engine built for replies, not opens

THE PROBLEM

Cold email to SMBs and funded startups was landing in spam or being ignored. Marketing-speak, heavy HTML and link-stuffed first sends were tripping filters and burning the domain before a human ever read a word.

WHAT I DID
  • Rebuilt the system around deliverability first: plain-text sends, no hyperlinks in early touches, and copy rules designed to read like a person wrote them
  • Wrote a four-touch sequence on a Hook, Pain, Shift, CTA framework with simple Yes/No reply triggers that make responding effortless
  • Layered psychology deliberately: mirroring, identity and micro-commitment across the sequence instead of one desperate pitch
  • Segmented targeting across US, UK, Canada and Australia/NZ, with separate angles per vertical
THE RESULT
4-touchsequenceevery send has one job and one ask
Plain textdeliverabilitybuilt to pass filters and feel human
4marketsUS · UK · CA · AU/NZ verticalised

SOLVED

Engineered the motion as an animated GIF review marquee instead, inside a hand-coded 480px HTML template, so the email moves in every major client.

Built chapter-based Mailchimp HTML templates and nurture flows that walk a new subscriber from curiosity to purchase, written like letters, not blasts.
